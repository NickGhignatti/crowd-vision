Goals

Welcome to the CrowdVision Developer Guide. Before diving into the codebase, it is crucial to understand what we are building and why the architecture is structured the way it is.

The primary goal of CrowdVision is to provide a robust, real-time crowd monitoring platform built on a digital twin paradigm. It serves facility managers by streaming live IoT sensor data directly into a 3D visualization of their buildings.
