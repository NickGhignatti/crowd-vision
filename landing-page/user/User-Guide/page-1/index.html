What is CrowdVision?

Welcome to the CrowdVision User Guide! 👋

💡 The Short Version CrowdVision is a comprehensive digital twin and crowd analytics platform. It is designed to help you monitor building occupancy, environmental conditions, and safety alerts in real-time.

Whether you are managing a single office floor or an entire campus, CrowdVision brings your physical spaces into the digital world, making it easier than ever to understand how your buildings are being used and to keep everyone inside them safe.

🌟 Core Capabilities

CrowdVision takes complex data and turns it into an intuitive, visual experience. Here is what you can do with the platform:

FeatureDescription
🏢 3D Digital TwinInteract with a live, 3D digital replica of your buildings and floors. See exactly what is happening, where it is happening.
📊 Analytics DashboardNavigate through beautifully designed dashboards to monitor real-time building occupancy and environmental conditions.
🔔 Smart NotificationsReceive instant alerts for critical events, such as overcrowding or high temperatures, so you can act immediately.
🤖 AI AssistantLeverage artificial intelligence to get quick answers and insights about your building’s data.
🛠️ Data ManagementEasily manage domains, buildings, and room data to keep your digital twin perfectly synced with the real world.

👥 Who is CrowdVision For?

CrowdVision is built to be accessible, yet powerful enough for different types of users:

  • Facility Managers: Get a bird’s-eye view of building health, optimize space utilization, and respond rapidly to environmental or occupancy alerts.
  • Administrators: Manage user access, configure building domains, and customize the digital twin environment.
  • General Users: View safe zones, check room availability, and stay informed about the spaces they interact with daily.

🔒 Access Note While some features of the platform can be viewed as a guest, administrative actions (like editing room details or managing domains) require a registered account.
